Responsive Images with max-width

The image below will shrink to fit smaller screens but will never stretch beyond its original size, preserving its quality. Try resizing your browser window to see it in action!

How It Works:

  • max-width: 100%; ensures the image never overflows its container.
  • height: auto; makes the browser calculate the height automatically to maintain the aspect ratio.
  • display: block; and margin: 0 auto; are used to center the image neatly on the page.
