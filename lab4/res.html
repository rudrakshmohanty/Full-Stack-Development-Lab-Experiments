Responsive Image Techniques

This demo shows the difference between using width and max-width to make images responsive. Resize your browser to see the effect!

Problem: width: 100%

This image uses width: 100%. On a large screen, it stretches beyond its original size, causing it to look pixelated and blurry.

Best Practice: max-width: 100%

This image uses max-width: 100%. It scales down perfectly but never grows larger than its original 400px width, preserving its quality.
